This Privacy Policy explains what data TailorBack ("we", "us") collects, why, and what we do with it. TailorBack is operated by [Company legal name], [jurisdiction]. For privacy questions or data requests, contact [support email].

1. Data we collect

  • Account details: your name and email address (via Google sign-in or email sign-up), a securely hashed password (email accounts only), email-verification status, and optionally your country and ZIP/postal code.
  • The content you submit: the CV/résumé text or files you upload and the job descriptions or job links you provide.
  • Generated content: the tailored résumé, cover letter, match score, and gaps we produce, plus basic metadata (which AI provider was used and how long generation took).
  • Billing data: a record of credit purchases and grants. Card payments are handled by Stripe; we receive confirmation and a Stripe session reference, not your full card number.
  • Technical data: standard server logs (e.g. request times) needed to operate and secure the Service.

2. How uploaded files are handled

When you upload a .pdf or .docx, we extract its text and then delete the uploaded file from disk immediately — we do not retain the original file. The extracted text is stored with your generation record so you can revisit and edit the result.

3. Third-party AI providers

To generate documents, your CV text and the job description are sent to one of the following AI providers (in fallback order, until one succeeds):

  • OpenAI
  • Google (Gemini)
  • Anthropic (Claude)

These providers process the text to return the tailored output. Their handling of data is governed by their own terms and privacy policies. We send only the CV text and job description needed for the task — not your name, email, or payment details — beyond what you have chosen to include in your CV text itself.

4. Other processors

  • Google OAuth — if you sign in with Google, Google provides us your email, name, and a user identifier.
  • Stripe — processes payments and sends us signed confirmation of completed checkouts.
  • [Hosting / email provider] — hosts the Service and, once configured, sends account-activation emails.

5. Why we use your data

  • To create your account and authenticate you.
  • To generate, store, and let you edit and download your documents.
  • To track free and paid generation credits and process purchases.
  • To operate, secure, debug, and improve the Service.
  • To send essential account emails (e.g. activation).

6. Retention

  • Generated document files (the downloadable .docx/.pdf) are deleted automatically after {{ retention_days }} days, or sooner if you delete them.
  • Your account, generation history (including stored CV and job-description text), and credit records are retained while your account is active, so you can revisit past generations.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting [support email].

7. Your rights

Depending on your location (for example under the EU/UK GDPR), you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact [support email]. You may also change your password and delete generated documents directly in your account settings.

8. Security

We use measures such as hashed passwords, account-scoped access to your documents, expiring download links, and signed payment webhooks. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your data.

9. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

10. International transfers

Because we use third-party AI and infrastructure providers, your data may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

11. Changes

We may update this Policy. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version; material changes will be highlighted in the app where appropriate.

12. Contact
