            The honest difference

            We tailor your real experience. We never make things up.

            Most tools happily pad a résumé with skills you don't have. TailorBack reframes what you've genuinely done to fit the job, and lists anything the role wants but your CV doesn't evidence as honest gaps, so you only walk into interviews you can actually back up.

            Questions, answered

            Will it lie or invent things on my résumé?

            No. TailorBack only uses the experience already in your CV; it reframes and reorders to match the job, and reports requirements you don't meet as gaps instead of fabricating them.

            Is my data safe?

            Your generated documents are private to your signed-in account and expire automatically. We don't sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for what we retain.

            Does it work with LinkedIn or Indeed job links?

            Company career portals (Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby) read reliably. LinkedIn & Indeed often block automated reading. If a link can't be read, you'll simply be asked to paste the text.

            What does it cost?

            Your first generations are free. After that, tailoring runs on one-time TailorBack Pro credit packs, with no subscription or monthly expiry.

            Can applicant tracking systems (ATS) read the output?

            Yes. The résumé uses standard section names, clean single-column formatting, and exports as .docx and PDF so parsers can read it.
